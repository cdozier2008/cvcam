About Us

Dirt2Klean provide kleaning services for residential, commercial and post construction. We are fast, efficient, and honest and customer satisfaction is our #1 goal. We will treat our clients as our family and this is extended to our services. We provide dependable kleaning services, such as Standard and Deep kleaning. Prices are based upon square foot. Our services are available for weekly, bi-weekly, monthly or one-time kleaning.

Our Services

Residential

There is nothing like coming home to a klean house. Dirt2Klean is here to help give your home the attention and care it deserves. When you hire us for your house kleaning, you will have the most complete, most thorough professional housekeeping services available! We customize the kleaning of your home to meet your needs and expectations. We promise to exceed the expectations of our clients whether you are moving in/out, just had a party with no time to klean, rental property is vacant and old tenants left a mess.
